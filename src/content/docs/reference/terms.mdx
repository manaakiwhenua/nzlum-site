Under this licence, you are free to:

- **Share** - copy and redistribute the material in any medium or format  
- **Adapt** - remix, transform, and build upon the material for any purpose, including commercial use

You must provide appropriate **attribution**, include a link to the licence, and indicate if changes were made. You may do so in any reasonable manner, but not in a way that suggests the funders or developers endorse you or your use.

## Data Usage and Liability

The NZLUM classification system is provided as-is and is intended for public-good use, including:

- scientific research  
- policy development and assessment  
- environmental reporting  
- planning and land management  
- education and capability building  



### Restrictions

There are no restrictions on commercial use beyond the requirements of the CC BY 4.0 licence. Users are responsible for ensuring that any derivative classifications or applications clearly identify modifications and do not imply official endorsement.


## Versioning and Future Updates
NZLUM follows a transparent versioning process, with update notes and documentation published alongside each release. Each version is registered under the DOI:  
[https://doi.org/10.26060/6MYG-2356](https://doi.org/10.26060/6MYG-2356)

Third-party datasets incorporated into NZLUM retain their original licences, which will be clearly noted in release documentation.

Ethical and culturally sensitive data considerations, including guidance for indigenous data use, will be added.
